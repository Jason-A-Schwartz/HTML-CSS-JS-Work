Determine The Type Of Videos You Will Make


It's important to figure out what kind of videos you want to make before you start purchasing or downloading things. This is because there are so many different kinds of videos you can make which may or may not require different materials. Here are a few examples of the many types of videos that you can create:


Virtual Logs
(Vlogs)		 Gaming Comedy Tutorials
Virtual logs "Vlogs" are videos in which you go out and record your daily adventures. Gaming videos have to do with recording/live streaming yourself playing your favorite video games. Comedy videos are really whatever you want them to be. However, they usually are scripted skits and jokes. Tutorial videos are based around the idea of teaching others how to do certain things.

You don't have to choose just one, you can create whatever kind of videos you want. You can make some videos about gaming and then make some about comedy. It's also possible to combine multiple types of genres such as:

  • "Gaming Tutorials"
  • "Comedic Vlogs"
  • "Vlog Tutorials"

The possibilities are endless as long as you want to create videos!
