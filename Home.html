Introduction

Welcome, this website is where you can find out everything you need to know when it comes to creating and editing videos/images that you want to upload to YouTube. This website is split into twelve easy steps to make getting started easy for everyone. Hopefully you find all the information that you are looking for, have fun!

  1. First you will learn about the inspiration needed to make videos and images for your content.
  2. Second you will learn about the software and hardware you need to download and purchase.
  3. Third you will learn how to use video editing software.
  4. Fourth you will learn how to use image editing software.
  5. Fifth you will learn how to create a YouTube account and upload videos to the site.
  6. Sixth you will learn some of the rules you must be aware of on YouTube.
  7. Seventh you will learn how to manage your time effectively.
  8. Eighth you will get suggestions for future video ideas.
  9. Ninth you will get suggestions for your channel name.
  10. Tenth you will learn when it is best to post videos.
  11. Eleventh you will learn what audience size to shoot for.
  12. Twelfth you will start planning your first video.
