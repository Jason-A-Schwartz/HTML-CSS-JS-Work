Rules And Regulations To Keep In Mind

Congratulations! You now know how to create, edit, and upload videos/images to YouTube. It may seem a little difficult now but it will get easier with trial and error and tons of experience. Now that you have finished the basics you should check out page two!


When it comes to uploading videos there are a few questions that you need to keep in mind:


Copyright
- Does your video violate YouTubes' copyright policy?
Community Guidlines
- Does your video follow YouTubes' community guidlines?
Age Restricted
- Is your video Age appropriate?
Monetization
- Will your video be Monetized?
