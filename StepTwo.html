Acquire The Materials

Once you figure out what kind of video you want to start with you can move onto the next step which is obtaining all the software and hardware you will need. Obviously, this list will vary based on what kinds of videos you plan on making. However, this is a detailed list of basic things you need to start.

Apple iMovie
- There are many video editing softwares out there. If you own a mac iMovie is free and easy to use. If not you can use another free program such as: Open Shot.

Adobe Photoshop
- Photoshop works cross platform and is the best software to use for editing images. However, it isn't free to use. Thankfully there are alternatives such as: Gimp.

GoPro
- GoPro's are small cameras that you can use to record vlogs or even be used as a face cam for gaming videos. They are the cheapest camera you could buy that delivers great quality.
